## ğŸ¨ Design Specifications

### Colors

**Background:**
- Main: `Color(white: 0.05)` - Very dark gray
- Cards: `Color.white.opacity(0.05)` - Subtle light
- Borders: `Color.white.opacity(0.1-0.15)` - Soft edges

**Text:**
- Primary: `.white` - Main content
- Secondary: `.white.opacity(0.6)` - Labels
- Tertiary: `.white.opacity(0.4)` - Hints

**Accents:**
- Gradients: Blue to Purple for CTAs
- Category colors: 11 distinct vibrant colors

### Typography

**Headers:**
- Large titles: 32-48pt, Bold/Heavy, Rounded
- Section headers: 15pt, Semibold, Uppercase
- Subsections: 13pt, Medium

**Body:**
- Item names: 14-16pt, Semibold
- Details: 12-13pt, Medium
- Labels: 11-12pt, Medium, Uppercase

**Numbers:**
- Amounts: 15-42pt, Bold, Rounded
- Percentages: 14pt, Semibold, Rounded
- Quantities: 14pt, Bold, Rounded

### Spacing

**Padding:**
- Cards: 16-24pt horizontal, 12-18pt vertical
- Sections: 20-32pt between
- Elements: 8-12pt within groups

**Corner Radius:**
- Large cards: 20-24pt
- Medium cards: 12-16pt
- Small buttons: 10-12pt

### Animations

**Timing:**
- Quick taps: 0.3s spring
- View transitions: 0.5s spring
- Chart animations: 1.0s spring

**Curves:**
- Spring response: 0.3-1.0
- Damping: 0.6-0.8
- Scale on press: 0.95-0.97
